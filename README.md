### *Predictive Academic Intelligence & Performance System*

---

## 🚀 Overview

**Buddhi AI** is an intelligent exam preparation system that transforms Previous Year Questions (PYQs) into **actionable insights, predictions, and personalized strategies**.

Unlike traditional study tools, Buddhi AI doesn’t just show what appeared before — it predicts **what will come next** and guides students on **what to focus on**.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Students today face:

* ❌ Random study patterns
* ❌ Unstructured PYQ practice
* ❌ No clarity on important topics
* ❌ Inefficient time utilization

Even after solving past papers, they still don’t know:

* What matters most
* What is likely to appear
* Where they are weak

---

## 💡 Solution

Buddhi AI converts exam data into **intelligent academic guidance** by:

* Understanding concepts (not just keywords)
* Predicting high-probability topics
* Structuring PYQs into meaningful clusters
* Generating adaptive study strategies
* Optimizing performance under time pressure

---

## 🧠 Core Innovation

### 🔍 1. Concept-Based PYQ Segregation (Game Changer)

> “We don’t organize questions — we organize knowledge.”

* Groups PYQs by **underlying concepts**, not keywords
* Identifies variations of the same question
* Enables **concept-level mastery**

📌 Example:

* “Explain Merge Sort”
* “Describe divide and conquer sorting”
  👉 Grouped into: **Merge Sort Concept**

---

### ⚡ 2. Exam Probability Engine

Predicts:

> “This topic has an 82% probability of appearing”

Based on:

* Frequency
* Recency
* Marks weight
* Trend patterns

---

### 📊 3. Probability Heatmap (Hero Component)

Visual dashboard showing:

* 🔴 High importance topics
* 🟡 Medium
* 🟢 Low

👉 Instantly highlights **where to focus**

---

### 📈 4. Trend Shift Detection

* Identifies rising topics 📈
* Detects declining trends 📉
* Predicts **future exam patterns**

---

### 🛡️ 5. Explainable AI (Evidence Layer)

Every prediction is backed by:

* Past questions

### 🎯 6. Adaptive Study Strategy Engine

Generates personalized plans based on:
